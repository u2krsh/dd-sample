About

Let the Magic Begin

Divine Dreams is one of its kind Party Banquet and Lawn synonymous with elegance and luxury delivered with utmost professionalism while maintaining high standards.

From the moment you make your way into the garden, our magical venue will take your breath away. With over 60,000 Hof lush outdoor lawn and over 6,000 sq.ft of magnificent indoor space in Delhi/NCR, Divine Dreams is a luxury venue like no other.

Here at Divine Dreams Luxury Farms & Banquets we help you in laying foundation for your dream events. Be it a wedding or any other celebration, we have carved a niche in setting a perfect way to herald new beginnings.

When you choose Divine Dreams, every element of your special day is taken care of to the finest detail. We pride ourselves in outstanding service, superior facilities and fine dining for a day planned to perfection and unique to you.

Our expert planners will help you bring your dream event to life and guarantees the utmost attention to detail - nothing is too much trouble. They will meet with you to discuss your individual requirements and put together the perfect package to suit you.
